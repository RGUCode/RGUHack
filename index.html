FAQ

What is RGUHack? RGUHack is an all-inclusive, free event organised for students by students. You will create teams with like minded people with a common aim; to create something awesome in just 24 hours.
Where will it be held? RGUHack will be held at Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen. For the past two years it has been held in the famous 'bear pit' at the Sir Ian Wood Building.
Is the event beginner friendly? It is absolutely no problem if you have never attended a hackathon before. You will most definitely not be the only one there. The main purpose of this hackathon is to learn something new, have fun and see how other people work.
What is MLH? MLH or Major League Hacking is the official student hackathon league. With their help we are able to organise this event and bring you some cool pieces of hardware, that you will be able to play with during the event.
Who can attend? You have to be a student currently in education. However, there is no limit to how young or old you are! You also don't have to be a computing student to come and contribute.
Can I sponsor the event? We would love to have you involved! Please use the form below to register your interest in sponsoring the event. The RGUHack team will be in touch with you.
